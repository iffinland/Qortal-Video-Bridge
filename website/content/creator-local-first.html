This website is meant to show what VideoBox Bridge actually produces in practice. A creator imports a source video, lets the local helper prepare it, and then publishes it into QDN.

Once the publish step is complete, the same video can be reused across chat posts, embed blocks, gallery pages, and future website sections without rebuilding the content from scratch.

For early outreach, this page works as a simple proof layer: people can read a short explanation, see a real playable example, and understand how the workflow looks before deciding whether they want to run the full creator tool themselves.
